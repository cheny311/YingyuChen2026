1 — How to play

Follow the on-screen prompts. Each scene tests a different “dating mechanic.”

Scene 0 — Enter the world: Be the one people are looking for.

Scene 1 — Shall we talk: Use dating app logic to decide how your message lands.

Scene 2 — Push and pulls: Play with the rhythm of interaction and boundaries.

Scene 3 — Timing test: Kiss with perfect timing to earn the Sunday date, or get dumped back into the loop.

Scene 4 — Wedding Photo Booth: Upload a photo or use your webcam, place yourself into a dramatic template, and save the final canvas.
