Dating Game — Notes

Dating Game is a playful p5.js mini-journey about dating as performance, timing, and luck—ending in a wedding photo booth you can actually save.

I built the scenes from my experience on dating apps in NYC: tuning yourself to match expectations, guessing signals, and looping back into the feed.

Each scene tests a different “dating mechanic”: matching a target vibe, negotiating distance, kissing with perfect timing, and finally posing for an over-the-top wedding template.

Press 1 anytime to jump to the next scene if you want to speed-run the story.
