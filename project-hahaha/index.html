1 — Concept

Laughter as pressure. The piece treats laughter like a trigger: automated, looping, sometimes out of sync with the body.

Drift and control. Videos are draggable; sounds shift as you move. You steer an unstable chorus.

Soft sculpture. The oversized face is both playful and uncanny—a cushion that watches and reacts.
